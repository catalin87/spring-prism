# Governance Model

Spring Prism operates under a **Founder-Leader model**.

## Leadership & Core Module Stewardship
- The original author holds final approval authority for all merges into the `spring-prism-core` module.
- This strict stewardship ensures the project’s vision remains unwaveringly focused on EU AI Act compliance, high-performance execution, and strict architectural decoupling without external corporate dilution.

## Contribution Flow & DCO
- All contributors must agree to the **Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)**. We do not use a CLA.
- Every commit must include a `Signed-off-by` line indicating that the contributor possesses the legal right to submit the code.
- Maintainers evaluate and merge features in external modules (`dashboard`, `starter`, `spring-ai`), but the `spring-prism-core` rules-engine remains under absolute Founder control.
