      How Spring Prism protects sensitive prompts

      1 Scan

      Prism intercepts outbound prompts, tools, or MCP payloads before they leave the trusted boundary.

      →
      2 Redact

      Detected PII is replaced with HMAC-backed Prism tokens that external systems cannot reverse.

      →
      3 Vault

      Original values remain inside the local or Redis-backed Prism vault, with bounded retention.

      →
      4 Restore

      Inbound responses are scanned for Prism tokens and restored only after signature validation succeeds.
